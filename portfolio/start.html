How I Got Here

Growing up, I've always been fascinated by technology.

If setting up installers and custom launchers counts, I started when I was just 5 years old!

I got started with learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at a young age through platforms like Google Grasshopper and Brilliant.

Then I got into anime, which led me to join Telegram groups where I discovered streaming and content management.

I started my own blog related to streaming and began posting content regularly.

That project closed soon after (I know it was immature of me), but it introduced me to cloud servers, Heroku hosting, and the fundamentals of streaming technology.

I had some weird experiences along the way. I was around 10 or 11 at that time.

I managed everything through Blogspot because I wasn't deep into CMS systems yet.

Around that time, my dad consulted with a company to get his online learning startup off the ground, but they undercut him and only provided a simple WordPress template with basic login functionality.

Then COVID hit our family hard. The isolation made me even lonelier, which pushed me deeper into coding as a way to cope and create.

During those difficult times, I helped him set up his online courses using Google Drive and simple access-sharing logic.

Now I'm working on it again, building it bigger and better than it ever was before.

And that's my story so far. Still learning, still building, and excited for what comes next.
